# Cold-Chain Handling SOP

Perishable and pharmaceutical goods must be maintained within the validated
temperature band for the entire journey.

- **Refrigerated (2–8 °C):** vaccines, biologics, fresh dairy. Any excursion
  above 8 °C for more than 30 minutes triggers a quality hold.
- **Frozen (-18 °C or below):** frozen foods, certain reagents.
- **Excursion response:** quarantine the affected pallet, log the sensor reading,
  and open a deviation ticket. Do not return product to inventory until QA signs off.

Temperature sensors report every 60 seconds. A sustained reading outside the band
for 3 consecutive intervals is treated as a confirmed excursion.
